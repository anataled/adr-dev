Who We Are

ADR Power Systems, Inc. was founded by David Arata and Richard DeBord in October of 2015. Operations began in a small warehouse near the Channel District in Tampa, Florida, and focused on marine transmission services for recreational and commercial fishing vessels ranging from small boats to mega yachts. Expert workmanship, coupled with rapid turn-around time, catapulted the company to broaden its presence to include the Gulf States, Great Lakes Region, Eastern Seabord and the Caribbean.

What We Do

Today, ADR Power Systems, Inc. represents global brands such as ZF Marine Transmissions, Baudouin Inboard Diesel engines, and Neander Motors outboard diesel engines. Our service offering also includes maritime electronic controls, marine engines, and industrial transmission repairs, among others. The company is composed of a balanced blend of skilled resources that act as a single-synchronized unit to minimize downtime and keep customers focused on their mission.
